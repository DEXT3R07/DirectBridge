Returns & Refunds

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If something isn't right, we're here to make it right.

Return Policy

  • Timeframe: You can return items within 14 days of delivery.
  • Condition: Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags attached.
  • Exceptions: Underwear, swimwear, and personalized items cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.
  • Damaged Items: If your item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos.

How to Return

  1. Log into your Dashboard and select the order.
  2. Click "Request Return" and fill out the form.
  3. Our team will review and approve within 24 hours.
  4. Ship the item back using our prepaid return label (for approved returns).
  5. Receive your refund within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.

Refund Methods

Refunds are processed to your original payment method. For bank transfer payments, refunds will be sent to the account you provide during the return process.
