Shipping Information

We deliver nationwide across Nigeria. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process.

Delivery Times

  • Lagos: 1-2 business days
  • Abuja, Port Harcourt: 2-3 business days
  • Other Major Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Remote Areas: 5-7 business days

Shipping Costs

  • Orders above ₦50,000: FREE shipping
  • Standard shipping: ₦2,500 flat rate
  • Express delivery: ₦5,000 (available in select cities)

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS and email with your tracking number. You can also track your order from your Dashboard.

Delivery Partners

We partner with reliable logistics companies including GIG Logistics, DHL, and local courier services to ensure your package arrives safely and on time.
