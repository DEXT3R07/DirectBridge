Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Payment

How do I place an order?
Browse products, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping details, and choose your payment method.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Paystack (Card, Bank Transfer, USSD) and manual bank transfers confirmed via WhatsApp.

Is my payment secure?
Yes. Paystack is PCI-DSS compliant and uses bank-grade encryption for all transactions.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?
1-2 days in Lagos, 2-5 days in other major cities, and 5-7 days for remote areas.

Can I track my order?
Yes! Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also track from your Dashboard.

Account

Do I need an account to shop?
You can browse without an account, but you'll need to sign up to place an order and track your purchases.

How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Still have questions?

Contact our support team and we'll be happy to help.
